Customer says

Just had lunch at the Fish House. Fast service. I had the fish of the day, hogfish snapper. It was excellent. Mild,flaky, light white fish which is seasonal and caught by spear. Had it deep fried on a tasty bun. Cole slaw was excellent and the French fries were fresh and hot. My husband had the fried grouper which I must say, was, the best grouper I have tasted in our 20 years of eating grouper. Lovely funky atmosphere. Large portions. Economically priced. We will return, for sure.

Rosemary G

We ate here after snorkeling in John Pennekamp. They were very accomodating with our attire--coverups over swimsuites, and seated us in the patio-which was fun! The waiter gave us the scoop on what his favorites, and customer favorites were. We had the Lion Fish Oscar Style and it was delicious. Key Lime pie was fantastic as well. Definitely will eat here again if we make it back to Key Largo

Milwaukeetravelbuffs

Great fresh fish, good service, wines, and surprisingly nicely cooked veggies made the meals here our best in Key Largo. Locals tell us it's the best, and after three meals, we believe them. They usually have invasive lionfish on the menu, delicious, and you do the waters a good turn by eating this dangerous invasive predator that tastes like grouper married with Dover sole!

Mary M
